EST. 1959

THE HERITAGE OF TASTE

On December 31st, 1959, our founder Chief Oluwole Adetoye signed a 99-year lease for our first brewery in Lagos. With a vision that transcended generations, he established what would become Nigeria's most beloved brewing tradition, creating a legacy that continues to shape the taste of our nation.

From those humble beginnings to becoming a national icon, our commitment to quality has never wavered.

CRAFTSMANSHIP

MASTERS OF BREWING

For over six decades, our master brewers have perfected the art of brewing. Using only the finest ingredients, our recipes combine traditional methods with modern innovation to create distinct flavors that have become part of Nigerian celebrations.

Every bottle represents our commitment to excellence and the meticulous attention to detail that has defined us since the beginning.

COMMUNITY

BREWING FOR NIGERIA

From Lagos to Abuja, from Kano to Port Harcourt, our brewery has been bringing Nigerians together. We've been part of weddings, naming ceremonies, milestone achievements, and everyday moments of joy across the nation.

Our commitment extends beyond brewing – we invest in local farmers, support community initiatives, and create economic opportunities throughout Nigeria.

INNOVATION

BREWING TOMORROW

While honoring our traditions, we continuously innovate. Our state-of-the-art facilities combine the latest brewing technology with sustainable practices, reducing our environmental footprint while enhancing the quality of our products.

From award-winning lagers to bold new craft selections, we're constantly exploring new flavors that resonate with both traditional beer lovers and the next generation of Nigerians.

OUR PROMISE

THE TASTE OF CELEBRATION

From our founder's vision to today's achievements, we continue to create beverages that bring Nigerians together. Our story is one of passion, resilience, and excellence – values that are infused in every drop we brew.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our heritage while embracing innovation, ensuring that Nigerian Brewery remains the taste of celebration for generations to come.

ESTABLISHED 1759

THE LEASE OF LIFE

On 31st December 1759, the man signed a NINE THOUSAND YEAR lease on St. James's Gate. If that's not belief, we don't know what is. And now we can see that his belief was a vision. From our legacy of philanthropy to how we have been taking care of our people for over 200 years.

SINCE 1820

MASTER BREWERS' HERITAGE

For generations, our master brewers have passed down the secrets of our unique brewing process. Each batch of our premium Nigerian beer is crafted with precision and care, using methods that have stood the test of time while embracing modern innovations to ensure exceptional quality.

LOCAL SOURCING

NIGERIAN INGREDIENTS

We take pride in our commitment to Nigerian agriculture, sourcing over 70% of our raw materials locally. Our partnerships with farmers across Nigeria have created sustainable livelihoods while ensuring the highest quality ingredients for our distinctive brews.

COMMUNITY FIRST

BREWING FOR NIGERIA

Beyond creating exceptional beers, we've built a legacy of community support across Nigeria. From our clean water initiatives to educational programs and healthcare support, we believe in brewing a better future for all Nigerians with every bottle we produce.

LOOKING FORWARD

While honoring our rich heritage, we continuously push the boundaries of brewing excellence. Our state-of-the-art facilities combine traditional brewing wisdom with cutting-edge technology, creating distinctive flavors that define the Nigerian beer experience for generations to come.
